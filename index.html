Frequently Asked Questions

🌤️ What’s the best time to visit Goa?

November to February is ideal for pleasant weather, beach parties, and festivals.

🛏️ Are there budget-friendly places to stay?

Yes! Hostels, homestays, and guesthouses are widely available for travelers.

🚗 Is it safe to rent scooters in Goa?

Yes, renting scooters is common. Always wear helmets and carry a valid license.

🍽️ What local dishes should I try?

Must-try dishes include Fish Curry Rice, Pork Vindaloo, Bebinca, and Xacuti.
